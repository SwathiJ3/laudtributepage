Personal Details

Born: 14 November 1889,Allahabad
Died:27 May 1964 (aged 74),New Delhi
Full name:Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru

Biography

Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru was the first Prime Minister of independent India Nehru was popularly known as Pandit Nehru. His affection for children made him lovingly called Chacha Nehru by kids. He was born on 14th November 1889 in Allahabad. His birthday is celebrated as the Children’s Day. He was an Indian independence activist and fought for India’s freedom along with other freedom fighters. He wrote an autobiography “Discovery of India” during his time in prison from 1942 to 1946. Later, in 1929 he was elected as the President of the Indian National Congress. India was freed from British rule in 1947.

         Award    

Bharat Ratna

On July 15th 1955, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, India’s first Prime Minister, was awarded the Bharat Ratna (India’s highest civilian honour) by then President Rajendra Prasad.
